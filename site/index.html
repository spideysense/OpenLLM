For developers

Two Lines to Switch

FreeLLM is a drop-in OpenAI replacement. Your existing code works today — just change the URL and key.

your_app.py
from openai import OpenAI

client = OpenAI(
    base_url="https://your-id.trycloudflare.com/v1",  # ← your public URL
    api_key="sk-freellm-a8f2k..."                     # ← from the app
)

response = client.chat.completions.create(
    model="gpt-4",  # routes to your local model
    messages=[{"role": "user", "content": "Hello!"}]
)

print(response.choices[0].message.content)

Works with LangChain, Cursor, Continue.dev, n8n, Zapier, AutoGen, and anything OpenAI-compatible.

🔒 Your model runs on your machine. The public URL just routes traffic to you — end-to-end encrypted via Cloudflare.

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about FreeLLM.

Is FreeLLM really completely free? +

Yes. The local version is free forever with no limits. You download the app, it runs AI models on your own hardware. No credit card, no trial, no hidden costs. Optional paid plans ($0.99–$1.99/mo) exist only if you want us to host it for you.

Does my data actually stay private? +

Yes, 100%. The AI model runs entirely on your CPU/GPU. Your conversations never leave your machine — not to us, not to Cloudflare, not to anyone. Zero telemetry. Zero data collection. You can inspect every line of code on GitHub to verify.

Can I replace ChatGPT or Claude with FreeLLM? +

For most tasks, yes. Models like Qwen 2.5 7B and Llama 3.3 70B rival GPT-4 and Claude for writing, coding, analysis, and Q&A. FreeLLM also gives you an OpenAI-compatible API — change two lines of code and your existing integrations work instantly.

What hardware do I need? +

Any Mac or Windows PC with 8GB+ RAM can run smaller models (3B–7B). For best results, 16GB RAM is recommended. Apple Silicon Macs (M1–M4) are exceptionally fast due to unified memory. NVIDIA GPU PCs also perform well. FreeLLM automatically detects your hardware and recommends the right model.

Does it work offline? +

Yes. Once a model is downloaded, FreeLLM works completely offline. No internet connection needed for AI inference. The only time you need internet is the initial model download and for the Cloudflare public URL feature.

How do I use FreeLLM in Cursor, Zapier, or my own code? +

FreeLLM runs an OpenAI-compatible API and gives you a public HTTPS URL. In any OpenAI SDK: set base_url to your FreeLLM URL and api_key to the key from the app. Works with LangChain, Cursor, Continue.dev, n8n, Zapier, AutoGen, and anything OpenAI-compatible.
