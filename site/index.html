For developers

OpenAI-compatible.
Two lines to switch.

Aspen exposes a standard OpenAI-compatible API. Change your base_url and api_key — two lines of code — and your entire stack runs privately on your own hardware, no data leaves — powered by localhost. Works with ChatGPT and Claude SDKs out of the box.

CursorVS CodeLangChainContinue.devn8nZapierAny OpenAI SDK
// Two lines. That's it.
import OpenAI from 'openai';

const ai = new OpenAI({
  baseURL: 'https://my.runonaspen.com/v1',
  apiKey:  'sk-aspen-...',
});

const r = await ai.chat.completions.create({
  model: 'qwen2.5:7b',
  messages: [{ role: 'user',
    content: 'Hello' }],
});
